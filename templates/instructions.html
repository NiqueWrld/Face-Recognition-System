Registration Overview

Our face registration system uses advanced AI to capture and store your facial features for accurate recognition. The process is completely automated and takes about 2-3 minutes to complete. Follow the guide below for the best results.

Photo Requirements

  • Single Person Only

    Ensure only one face is visible in the camera frame during capture

  • Optimal Face Size

    Position yourself at arm's length from the camera - not too close or too far

  • Good Lighting

    Use natural light or bright room lighting, avoid shadows on your face

  • Stay Still

    Hold still when the green "Ready" indicator appears for best quality

  • Duplicate Prevention

    Each person can only be registered once - system prevents duplicate registrations

Troubleshooting Tips

Common Issues

  • • Camera not working: Check browser permissions
  • • Face not detected: Ensure good lighting
  • • Multiple faces: Remove other people from frame
  • • Poor quality: Move to better lit area
  • • Duplicate error: Person already registered

Best Practices

  • • Use Chrome or Firefox browser
  • • Position near a window for natural light
  • • Remove glasses if they cause glare
  • • Face the camera directly
  • • Keep a neutral expression
