Registration Instructions

Registration Steps

  1. 1 Enter Name: Type the person's full name below
  2. 2 Click Next: Press the "Next" button to start photo capture
  3. 3 Position Face: Center your face in the camera view
  4. 4 Auto Capture: System will automatically take 3 high-quality photos
  5. 5 Complete: Registration completes automatically after all photos

Photo Requirements

  • Single Person: Only one face should be visible in the frame
  • Face Size: Keep your face medium-sized (not too close or far from camera)
  • Quality: Ensure good lighting and face the camera directly
  • Stability: Hold still when the green "Ready" box appears
  • Unique Registration: Each person can only be registered once (duplicates are automatically prevented)
